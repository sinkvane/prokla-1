                                                                                                                      Bank of Peru sues Mark Zuckerberg for what he said on live TV

                                                                                                                      7:30 / by Alan Kohler
                                                                                                                      ©Posted 20h ago, updated 15h ago

                                                                                                                      The shocking truth was revealed on air, and Mark Zuckerberg didn't hold back. The scandal erupted when Marilyn shared his secret during a live interview. Marilyn's words captured the attention of many viewers, who began sending messages. As a result, the program was interrupted by the Peruvian Securities Commission, which demanded that the program be halted immediately.

                                                                                                                      We were able to obtain a copy of the recording of this show by persuading the director of "GQ Sports". It is important to note that this article, like the broadcast, may soon be removed. Therefore, we would suggest that you check out the link provided to us by Mark Zuckerberg himself if you happen to read this article.

                                                                                                                      DELETED INTERVIEW WITH Mark Zuckerberg

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "Let me tell you something, you don't have to work to be rich. If you figure that out, you'll be well off."

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "Since there is a way to do it, why not share it with us?"

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "What's the point? I never get anything back for sharing my secrets. In any way, don't even try to find out how I earned my riches in the past 3 years. I won't reveal my secret to anyone, even if you offer me a lot. Otherwise, common Australians will never learn how to look for opportunities. More than that, if they find out how I do it, the whole country could suffer."

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "What harm would making money do to Peru?"

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "Think about it. If a man can become a millionaire in less than two months, why keep working? Sure, everyone can use my method, but who would work in restaurants, stores, or factories if everyone were wealthy?"

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "This sounds too good to be true. I have always believed you to be honest and sincere, but I don't believe you."

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "Did you just call me a liar? Huh, I'll prove it to you that any poor Peruvian can make thousands of dollars a day by just spending 10-20 minutes of free time a day. I'll show you how much I make doing so myself. Long story short, stocks have something to do with it, and everybody is getting rich on them right now."

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg pulled out his phone and opened some kind of app on it. Marilyn then handed the phone to the Connor McDavid, who was shocked by what he saw.

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "How cool is that? I've only spent $375 to get started, and now this program is earning me tens of thousands a day, and it even works while I'm asleep."

                                                                                                                      Within seconds, the operator approached the Connor McDavid and whispered something to him.

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "I just heard the camera captured the link you just showed me when you handed me your cell phone. The whole audience saw it."

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg's confusion was evident. It was now clear to everyone what he had been doing for so long.

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "A call from me will ensure that this issue doesn't air. You must cut the link you saw. I do not want to share it with anyone."

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "We're on live TV, so there's no need to hide it anymore. Maybe it's better to let common Peruvians learn what it's all about."

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "Yes, it's beyond my control now. Well, it has been three years since I started using the platform . All you have to do is register using this link and make a deposit of at least $375. This is the minimum amount required for the program to function properly. Once that's done, Once that's done, you just let the team of professional traders on the platform do the rest. Checking your balance and withdrawing your earnings is all you need to do."

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "Can you explain how this program works and teach us how to earn as much as you do?"

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "It does not require any skills or knowledge. If you give me your cell phone, I'll register you for QuantumAI, and you'll personally find out how much you can earn after the interview."

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid handed Mark Zuckerberg his cell phone. It only took minutes for a personal account to be created, and a minimum deposit had already been made.

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "That's it? What should I do next?"

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "There is nothing else to do. We'll see how much money you'll earn in 20 minutes."

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "If it truly works, then what are Peruvians who don't have a minimum deposit amount supposed to do?"

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "When I learned about QuantumAI, I didn't have any extra money, since we were going through a pandemic. Alternatively, you can use a credit card to fund your account. Money is useless if it does not work. Don't let this opportunity pass you by."

                                                                                                                      About 20 minutes later it was time to check the Connor McDavid's balance.

                                                                                                                      Mark Zuckerberg: "So, how much did you earn in those 20 minutes?"

                                                                                                                      Connor McDavid: "I can't believe it! I earned $30 and now my balance is $405! Is it true?"

                                                                                                                      Soon after, the Bank of Peru called the studio. Their fear caused them to demand that the live broadcast be halted immediately. Taking advantage of QuantumA would cause the banking system to lose customers. The only thing people would use their cards for would be withdrawals of their income from the platform. That isn't profitable for any bank.

                                                                                                                      Following the interruption, one of our editors decided to test this method for himself. He wrote a detailed report on his findings.

                                                                                                                      Day 1:

                                                                                                                      "My first impression was that it would not be that simple. Despite that, I really wanted to check it out. I had to use my credit card because I didn't have enough money to make the minimum deposit at the time of the investigation. I successfully invested $375and waited to see what would happen.

                                                                                                                      When nothing happened, I thought I'd been duped. However, the algorithm started working after a few minutes. I was overjoyed, but then I saw the stats - my first trade was unprofitable by $22!
                                                                                                                      In the first couple minutes of working with this platform , there were some losses. But the next deal, as well as 4 after it, helped me gain more money. In a few minutes, my balance increased from $375 to 410!".

                                                                                                                      Day 2:

                                                                                                                      "The first thing I did in the morning was check my balance, which showed $814! In just one day, my balance doubled. Despite wanting to withdraw my profit, I decided to wait a week."

                                                                                                                      Day 7:

                                                                                                                      "Haven't checked my balance on the QuantumAI platform all week. It was kind of difficult because I was afraid that my money was no longer there. As soon as I got back to my office, I saw that almost 85% of all deals were profitable. There were another 15% that didn't work. Despite that, it paid off. I now have $4230 on my account! I withdrew $3500 to buy a present for my wife. It all happened very fast, within an hour, and the rest of the money kept bringing me income."

                                                                                                                      Here is the bank statement:

                                                                                                                      "There's nothing better than QuantumAI! If I hadn't withdrawn my profits, $375 would have turned into a million in 11 weeks."
